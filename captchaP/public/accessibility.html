CAPTCHA Accessibility Challenges

CAPTCHAs are designed to distinguish humans from bots, but many systems rely on visual or audio tasks that can exclude users with disabilities. Common challenges include:

  • Image-based CAPTCHAs are often inaccessible to individuals with visual impairments.
  • Audio CAPTCHAs may not serve users who are deaf or hard of hearing.
  • Even alternative methods can fall short for users with cognitive or multiple disabilities.
  • Lack of customizable accessibility options in traditional CAPTCHA designs.

Our research explores these challenges and investigates inclusive alternatives that ensure robust security without compromising accessibility, so every user—regardless of ability—can navigate the digital world seamlessly.
