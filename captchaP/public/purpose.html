Purpose of CAPTCHAs

CAPTCHAs (Completely Automated Public Turing tests to tell Computers and Humans Apart) are designed to distinguish human users from automated bots. They help protect websites by preventing spam, brute force attacks, and other malicious activities.

However, traditional CAPTCHAs often create significant accessibility challenges for users with disabilities. Our research explores these challenges and investigates innovative, inclusive alternatives that maintain security while improving user accessibility.
